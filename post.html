What if the robot could talk?

Posted 04/10/2019 by Robert Jackson

Okay so, I had an idea the other day when working with the robots and I thought to myself - what if the robot made sounds too? If there was a sound to each Tagtile, it would be interesting to learn the kids to memorize patterns like if their eyes are closed. Imagine this exercise - one student closes his/her eyes and another student creates a path with the Tagtiles, then the student with his/her eyes closed has to listen and then recreate the same pattern and guess. What do you think? What else could sound add?
